The Hawa Mahal is a palace in the city of Jaipur, Rajasthan, India. Built from red and pink sandstone, it is on the edge of the City Palace, Jaipur, and extends to the Zenana, or women's chambers.

Ayodya Ram Mandir

The Ram Mandir, or Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Mandir, is a Hindu temple complex in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, India. The temple is dedicated to Lord Rama, a principal deity of Hinduism, and is believed to be located at the site of his birthplace. The temple was inaugurated on January 22, 2024.
